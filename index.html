a letter

For Prachi

with everything

They asked me — why do you love her so much.
What's so special about her.

I sat down. Stayed quiet for a few minutes.
And then I said —

You just know. There doesn't have to be a reason.
Your heart chooses her before your mind even gets a say.

And my heart chose Prachi.

✦

I want to talk to you because you're the first person I think of when something happens — good or bad. My mind just automatically goes, "I should tell her this."

I want to talk to you because even your simple "hmm" or "okay" feels different. It's not just a message. It's you. And somehow, that makes it important.

I want to talk to you because when you disappear for a while, it actually feels weird. Not empty… but like something is missing that should be there.

I want to talk to you because you're not perfect — and that's exactly why you're real to me. Your moods, your random "baat nhi karni hai? bolda sath sath" the moment I don't reply… your silly talks, your soft side. I like all of it.

I want to talk to you because you make normal conversations feel special. We can talk about nothing, and it still doesn't feel like nothing.

I want to talk to you because I like the way you care… even when you pretend you don't.

I want to talk to you because when I'm talking to you, I don't feel like I have to be someone else. I'm just… me.

I want to talk to you because you became a habit I never want to break. Not a bad habit… but the kind that feels like comfort. The kind that feels like home.

✦
You're the kind of girl who looks down when I stare at you a little too long. Whose smile isn't always real. Who cries herself to sleep sometimes and cares a little too much about what I say.

But you're also the same girl who loves with everything you have. Who makes every conversation feel warm. Who leaves everything around her a little better than before.

so they asked me — why her?

It's not 1000 reasons.
It's just this one truth —

Out of everyone,
my heart chooses you, Prachi.
Again and again. Every single day.

You're the kind of girl who will give me your heart.
And I promise —
I'll handle it with care.

always yours ❤
